About Screen Snapshots

Windows provides the Snipping Tool to enable you to take a "snipshot" of a portion of the screen.  With it, you can show small portions of your screen as part of a lab report.

  1. Arrange your screen the way you want, and activate the desired window.
  2. Activate the Snipping Tool from the start menu.
  3. Drag the mouse over the portion of the screen that you wish to capture.
  4. Click the Copy button (third icon, two pieces of paper).
  5. Switch to your lab report, move to the location where you want to place the image and Paste it in.
  6. Clicking the New button in the Snipping Tool will enable you to take another snipshot!
